Welcome to Oregon Tournament 2024!

An open rank draft tournament for current and former Oregonians!

Format

Double Elimination 3v3 TS3 Captain Draft with 8-12 teams.

  • The top 8-12 players in qualifiers will draft from the remaining players to create teams.
  • Players will be seeded by their average rank on the maps in qualifiers.
  • Your average score in qualifiers must be at least 10% of the maximum possbile to qualify.
    • If less than 24 players qualify, the qualification threshold will become null.
    • If less than 30 players qualify, the number of teams will be 8.
    • If 30 or more players qualify, the number of teams will expand to 10.
    • If 36 or more players qualify, the number of teams will expand to 12.

Match Rules

  • Scheduling

    • After default match times are assigned, the captains of each team may communicate with their opponents to decide upon a new match time.
    • Once a new time has been decided, submit a screenshot of the agreement to the #reschedules channel in the discord.
    • As a last resort, in the case that there is no time during the weekend or the week before or after the match that works, the teams may agree to play the match as a 2v2.

  • Delays

    • If the rescheduling deadline has passed, and you are not able to make an agreed or default match time, communicate with the referee, host, and opposing team and agree upon either a new time for the match or a maximum delay before the match begins.
    • If there is no communication from your team for…
      • 5 minutes, you forfeit the roll.
      • 10 minutes, you forfeit your bans.
      • 15 minutes, you forfeit the match.

  • Mods

    • ScoreV2 and NF on all maps, HD optional on Tiebreakers.

  • Warmups

    • Each team may choose a warmup with a maximum length of 3 minutes.

  • Rolls

    • Roll winner chooses either pick or ban order.
    • Roll loser chooses the other order.

  • Bans

    • Ban order will be A-B-B-A.
    • No double bans, except in the NM pool.

  • Picks

    • No pick restrictions.
    • Each pick will have a timer of 120 seconds. Exceeding this timer will result in one of your timeouts being used. If you have no timeouts remaining, a random map among those remaining will be chosen.

  • Timeouts

    • Each team will be allowed two 90 second timeouts per match.

  • Disconnections

    • If a player disconnects within 30 seconds of a map starting, it will be aborted. This will be allowed a maximum of twice per match, per team.
    • If a player disconnects after 30 seconds of a map starting, or after exceeding the abort limit, the map will continue, and the score of the disconnected player may be added to the team's score if proof is presented swiftly.
      • Proof may be either a Shift+F12 screenshot uploaded to the osu! website of the score screen with a matching timestamp, or a clip of the stream if the match is live.

Qualifiers Procedure

  • You have one attempt at a qualifiers lobby.
  • There will be one group lobby at 6PM each Saturday and Sunday of qualifiers.
  • Qualifier lobbies may be self-hosted. You MUST follow this exact procedure.
    • Create a multi lobby. It may be named anything.
    • Before ANY maps have been played, DM Rozemyne (@wrongwayscout) on discord the MP link to your lobby.
    • Turn on ScoreV2 as the Win Condition in the bottom right.
    • Turn on NoFail and do not remove it at any point.
    • Use “!mp start” to begin the match.
    • Play through all the maps in the Qualifiers pool once in order with the correct mods.

Draft Procedure

  • The captains will choose players starting in reverse order of seed, so lowest seeded captain picks first, first seeded captain picks last.
  • Once the first seeded captain picks, the order will reverse, and the first seeded captain will pick again, as the order proceeds back to the lowest seeded captain, who will pick last.
  • The captains will have 3 minutes to make each pick, but it would be in their best interest to prepare a drafting strategy beforehand.
  • Any captain who is unable to make the scheduled draft time may either submit an ordered list of every qualifying participant or choose to have the host draft whomever is the highest seed available on their picks.
